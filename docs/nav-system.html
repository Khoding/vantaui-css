Chrome & Navigation System

A header and footer system, plus three alternatives to the side rail — a horizontal top-nav, an off-canvas drawer on the native <dialog>, and a bottom command bar. Semantic HTML first, vui-* variants on top, responsive by default. The bar above is the live top-nav; resize the window to watch it collapse into the burger + drawer.

The side rail is one option. Here are three more — pick the one that fits the surface.

Off-canvas drawer<dialog>

A native modal <dialog class="vui-drawer">. Opens with showModal() or an invoker command; animates in with @starting-style + allow-discrete. Esc and backdrop-click close it.
